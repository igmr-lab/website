Image-Guided Medical Robotics Lab

Mark Draelos, MD, PhD

Principal Investigator

I am an Assistant Professor of Robotics, Ophthalmology & Visual Sciences and Biomedical Engineering (by courtesy) at the University of Michigan. My research group studies medical robotics, intrasurgical navigation, biomedical imaging, data visualization, medical device development, and real-time algorithms.
